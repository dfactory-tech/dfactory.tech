01 / About us

Dfactory Tech is a distributed fintech company building at the intersection of open systems and global finance.

Dfactory Ltd develops decentralized financial technology that helps people manage risk and gain exposure to specific assets through transparent, non-custodial infrastructure.

Our systems are designed to be compatible and composable with the broader Web3 ecosystem—creating foundations that others can build on.
